Our Story

Your Local Wellington Sparky

PowerLine Electrical is a family-owned business proudly serving Wellington and the Hutt Valley for over 15 years. Founded by Dave Mitchell, a born-and-bred Wellingtonian, we've built our reputation on honest work, fair pricing, and turning up when we say we will.

We're not a faceless corporation — we're your neighbours. From a simple plug installation to a full commercial fit-out, every job gets the same attention to detail and the same commitment to safety. That's our promise.

EWRB Registered

Licensed with the Electrical Workers Registration Board of New Zealand. Every electrician on our team holds a current practising licence — your guarantee that the work is done to the highest standard and meets all NZ regulatory requirements.

🏆

Master Electricians NZ

As proud members of Master Electricians New Zealand, we adhere to a strict code of practice. This membership means independent oversight, guaranteed workmanship, and access to a disputes resolution process if you ever need it.

📋

Safety Certificate Every Job

Every job we complete comes with an electrical safety certificate — it's not optional, it's how we operate. This certificate confirms the work has been tested and is safe and compliant with New Zealand electrical regulations.

✅

Fully Insured

We carry full public liability insurance and professional indemnity coverage. This protects you, your property, and our team on every single job. We can provide certificates of currency on request.
